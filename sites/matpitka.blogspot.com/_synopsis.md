## The TGD Framework for Free Energy

Pitkänen's explanation for over-unity and energy anomalies rests on three pillars:

### 1. Dark Matter as h_eff = n × h₀ Phases
In TGD, dark matter is NOT a new particle — it's ordinary matter in phases where Planck's constant is effectively larger (h_eff = n × h₀). These phases exist on separate "sheets" of many-sheeted space-time. Energy can transfer between sheets in ways that APPEAR to violate conservation laws when measured on a single sheet, but conserve energy across all sheets.

### 2. Time Mirror Mechanism (Remote Metabolism)
Negative energy signals can travel backward in time (in zero energy ontology). A system can send a negative energy signal to the past, which is received as positive energy in the geometric past. This creates an apparent energy gain in the sender's frame. Pitkänen calls this "remote metabolism" — borrowing energy from a distant source via time-reversed signals.

### 3. Topological Field Quantization
Maxwell's fields decompose into topological quanta — **magnetic flux tubes** — which are actual 4D space-time surfaces, not just field configurations. These flux tubes carry dark matter phases, mediate interactions between sheets, and explain phenomena from water memory to cold fusion to rotating magnetic system anomalies.

**Archive bridge:** This is NOT the same as the CEM/EE framework (Bearden), but arrives at a compatible conclusion from opposite direction: standard Maxwell EM is incomplete because it cannot represent multi-sheeted topology or variable Planck constant. TGD adds structure FROM ABOVE (new geometry), CEM/EE argues structure was REMOVED from below (Heaviside/Lorentz simplification). Both conclude: the standard model misses real phenomena.
