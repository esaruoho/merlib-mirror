 SPACE
 GEOMETRY

 dimensional evolving structures, as shown in this
 drawing, serve as an introduction on how to view a
 structure or skeleton formation on which energy
 (motion) of different dimension can find expression
 in order to bring about endless complex effects on
 a physical awareness level. It is meant to
 illustrate the possibility of INWARD awareness
 levels. Keep in mind that this is only meant to be
 a graphic demonstration as perceived by the writer
 of the interweaving of INNER spaces (dimensions) as
 they occur throughout the universal mind or the
 universe of cause and effect. This is the
 foundation of thought machinery, the manifestation
 of form through which the essence mirrors itself
 for ever in eternity. Thought in itself is an
 illusion because it takes the essence and creates
 form out of essence and therefore distorting the
 only reality which is pure essence. 

 Man is made from and operates
 through this vortex thought machinery within the
 limitations imposed upon by himself. In other
 words, everything in this universe of cause and
 effect creates itself with man being in between
 somewhere with his recorded actions and reactions
 becoming his destiny through which he or she learns
 to become ONE with the cause less cause and leave
 behind this illusive thought process
 altogether.
