You can make a difference

If you feel that what we focus on is important, you can make a difference by supporting us financially. Giving a voluntary donation will help us take another step with our research and publication efforts. Your help is gratefully appreciated and will make a difference.

Institute of Ecological Technology (Stiftelsen Institutet för Ekologisk Teknik) is a non-profit foundation and distributed self-organizing research institute, situated in Sweden.

On-line donations

You can make a donation from your PayPal account. If you do not have a PayPal account you can make a donation securely via PayPal from your credit card.
