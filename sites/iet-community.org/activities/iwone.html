The IWONE conferences
a
The International Workshop on Natual Energies (IWONE) is a workshop covering non-traditional ways to affect water flow, water quality, plants, weather and eco-systems, as well as non-traditional alternative energy sources. Historically the workshop has had a focus on ideas related to the Austrian naturalist Viktor Schauberger.

The workshop is organized every two years by Institute of Ecological Technology (IET). Since 2003 the workshop takes place at Höör (near Malmö) in southern Sweden.

The next IWONE conference will take place on August 11th - 13th, 2023.
