       IWONE 2003


2nd International Workshop On Natural Energies
(8.8 - 10.8 2003 in Höör near Malmö, Sweden)




Invitation

After the first and successful IWONE in Leipzig we are now happy to follow up with the next meeting in Sweden. IWONE is a platform for individuals and groups concerned with Natural Energies , for instance in connection with the ideas of Viktor and Walter Schauberger.
As these people live and work in different countries, separated by long distances and contact by internet etc. is not always sufficient or efficient enough it is necessary to meet "in real life" so that information can be exchanged and discussions can be done face to face.

The participants should hold short speeches (15 or 30 minutes) in order to present new ideas, inform about their actual work or recent research. On the other hand they will get the best feedback through discussions and stimulation s by the audience. Time will be reserved for discussions of the speeches. Special time for discussion of chosen topics will also be reserved during one of the evenings.

Title and abstract of the speech, presentation or demonstration (in some few sentences) have to be sent, in paper or electronic format, preferred by the end of April but no longer until 15:th of May, 2003. Please add any special requirements for equipment. A detailed summary (0,5 - 5 pages) of the same must be sent by the end of June 2003. We will try to maintain a balance between speeches that cover theoretical, experimental and practical results. The speeches will be distributed in written form to all participants after the different speeches.

A confirmation of your registration will be sent by email to you, after having received your booking and when the number of participants is definitive.

Maximum number of participants: 45

*    The proposed agenda could change due to number of participants. The final agenda will be published on the web during July (see the agenda link).

You are all very welcome!

Curt Hallberg, IET
