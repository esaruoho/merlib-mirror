How to get  here

The seminar is going to take place in the city of Höör situated in the centre of Skåne aprox.
70 km north east of Malmö.

Nearest international aiport is Kastrup (Copenhagen). From Kastrup, train leaves (starts in Copenhagen) every 20 minute to Malmö railway station and further on to Höör.
In Höör I can meet up for the last kilometres, please contact me via email some week before the seminar.

Train direct to Malmö (or Copenhagen) then as from the airport.
Time schedule for the Kustpilen trains

Driving can be done
1)    From Germany via the ferries from Sassnitz, Rostok or Travemünde to Trelleborg.
        Then via road 108 towards Lund and north via E 22.
        After Hurva turn left towards Hässleholm on road 23. After aprox 30 km you will pass through Höör.
         Link to TT-line

2)    From Denmark via the bridge (fee 275 SEK aprox. 30 euro one direction) to (around)
        Malmö then via E 22 and further on as in case 1.
