Mailing list
Exchange ideas over our mailing list on "self-organization technology" (a name we consider being appropriate for the phenomena we are studying). The word is mostly free if you use the normal "netiquette". All in all, the topics for this list are the same as those you can find in the About IET and Research sections.

We do not wish to emphasize only the work and ideas related to Viktor Schauberger. When it comes to developing a natural technology, there exist many other interesting ideas as well. But the idea of studying nature and attempting to learn from and copy the way it works is quite essential.

To subscribe to the mailing list, send an email to:

self-org-tech-request@iet-community.org

with the word subscribe in the message body (not as subject - no subject is needed). Later on when you are sending email to the list, just use the following email address:

self-org-tech@iet-community.org
