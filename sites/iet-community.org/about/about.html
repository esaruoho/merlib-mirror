What we are - and our vision
a
The Institute of Ecological Technology is a distributed self-organizing research institute, with a focus on non-conventional ideas and solutions within ecological technology. Our focus is on practical applications and on becoming aware of our interconnectedness with nature.

History
a
The Institute of Ecological Technology was formed in the late 1970s, to be a platform for research and projects in alternative science, and has undergone a series on metamorphoses since.
It traces its roots to the "study and copy nature" perspective that had been advanced by the Austrian Viktor
Schauberger about half a century earlier.
