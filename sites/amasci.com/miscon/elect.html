  • 4. ELECTRICAL ENERGY FLOWS IN A CIRCLE?
  • 5. DURING ELECTRIC CURRENT, ELECTRONS JUMP FROM ATOM TO ATOM?
  • 6. ELECTRIC POWER FLOWS FROM GENERATOR TO CONSUMER?

  • 9. ELECTROMAGNET COILS USE ENERGY TO CREATE MAGNETISM?
