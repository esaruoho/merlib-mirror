"Cold Fusion" Links

Cold Fusion is not normal fusion; it produces helium but lacks gammas. A more appropriate name for it is "Low Energy Nuclear Reactions," LENR, or "Chemically-Assisted Nuclear Reactions," CANR... or CMNS "Condensed Matter Nuclear Science."
