MAGLEV CRADLE DIAGRAM
W. Beaty 1994


MAGLEV CRADLE ARTICLE:   Page 1  | Page 2  | Page 3  | Page 4

Here a 3 inch alnico rod magnet is levitated by the cradle. Below the magnet are two rows of tilted "null coils.) On the core face of each coil is the Hall device which provides the feedback-generated repulsion profile between the magnet and the coil. This particular version was only marginally stable, and the bar magnet would remain levitated for several seconds before oscillations built up and ejected it from the device.

See also: Schematic & more info
