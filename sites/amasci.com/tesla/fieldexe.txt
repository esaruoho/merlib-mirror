Ed Harris wrote and compiled a program for the PC.  Here's a blurb
he wrote for it and the uuencoded .exe.  

Chip

	I wrote up some programs which solve laplace's equation for the 
purpose of visualizing the electric fields around tesla coils of various 
shapes with or without torroids (or whatever one wants). I was wondering if 
you think people on the list would be interested enough that I should post 
a copy of the program.

	The program grapicaly plots the voltage distribution around the 
coil and terminal. With it, one can eaily see the good/bad effects of 
torriod placement. One can also see where, approximately, the initial 
breakdown is likely to occur. It does not, at this time, show what happens 
to the elcetric fields after breakdown.
