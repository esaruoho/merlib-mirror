 > How exactly does one ballast a "pole pig" or other large       
 > commercial transformer? 

I have always found a few Magic Chef oven elements and an arc
welder in series or series/parallel with the transformer low
voltage winding worked extremely well. A "salt water rheostat"
can be built in a plastic waste can doped with a few teaspoons of
baking soda. Current is controlled by raising and lowering copper
electrodes into the water bath.
