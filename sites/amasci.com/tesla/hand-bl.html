I actually made something called "BALL LIGHNING", yes twice in a row. I've 
seen the video on uTube ,but it was nothing like what I experienced.  It 
took me ten years to actually find out what it was...HOW I CAME ACROSS 
THIS "NATURAL PHENOMENON? THE STORY THAT YOU ARE ABOUT TO READ, IS REAL. 
THIS HAPPENED TO ME IN THE LATE 1980'S. I NEED TO FIRST TELL YOU OF THE 
POSITION WITH ALL THE ELEMENTS THAT WERE AROUND ME. THE HOUSE WAS BUILT 
WITH ALL WOOD, AND THAT WOULD KEEP THE ELECTRCITY TRAPED INSIDE. THE 
AMOUNT OF FRICTION FROM MOVEMENT, ADDED TO THE ROOMS ELECTRICTY. THE LARGE 
AMOUNT OF ELECTRICTY THAT SPILLED INTO THE ROOM FROM THE DRYER. PLUS MY 
INTEREST OF CURIOSITY, TO LEARN MORE ABOUT LIFE THAT IS AROUND ME. ALSO MY 
DESIRE OF HOPING THAT SOMEONE WOULD BE INTERESTED AND HAVE USE FOR WHAT I 
CAME ACROSS. : 





I was taking dancing lessons at my dance teachers home with a class of 
10-15 other ladies. It was in the hills of Berkley, California. The front 
room was large, a very clean room, which had, a wood celling, wood walls, 
and a wood floor that was underneath the short pile Asian rug that she had 
covered her floor with. We moved the furniture off of the rug so there 
would be enough room for all of us to dance around. After the dance class, 
then, we were to sleep over. We shuffled our feet on the rug for about 3-4 
hours, dancing pretty much straight through. 





Teacher had asthma so she had 
three layers of curtains on each window and doors. All curtains were at 
least 3" away from in front of each other and would meet to the floor in 
length. Also 3 layers of curtains were even on the entrance frame to the 
kitchen. There was two windows that were across from each other that were 
small. Those windows were 2 paned windows, about 5' up from the floor. The 
front door was a double-door made out of wood, there was a double glass 
door to walk through after the front door and a kitchen frame entrance.  





There were 3 layers of curtains in front of each one of those places. So 
the room itself felt warm, very dry, dust free, and draft free. The 
laundry room did not have the curtains pulled together, they were 
completely pushed to the side so that room was all the way open. That room 
was connected to the front room. Teacher took all the blankets that had 
already been washed, put them into the dryer without any antistatic 
squares that a person would usually buy for inside the dryer. And again 
because of her asthma she never did that. So as she was pulling out the 
blankets you could hear and see the static popping and flashing about on 
the blankets because the lights were turned off.






We were lined up to take the blankets then lay down to sleep on the floor 
with washed and dried pillow cases and pillows.  Everybody fell asleep 
fast. To let you know I wouldn't express this next action not unless it 
was a true natural phenomenon that happened. I am a true ribbon rubber 
with the end of my fingertips laying flat, also rubbing the ribbon in 
between my fingers. It helps me to relax. Soooo, I began to rub the soft 
silk, thick ribbon. (It was a wide and thick sewed ribbon strip, around 
the blanket) Static sparks began to pop up off of the ribbon and a lot of 
them. It was like a light show, beautiful. By the way, the season was 
sometime in the winter and we danced into the evening around up to 8:00pm. 
We all settled down very shortly after we received our blankets. It was 
dark out, with an overcast sky....  






After I was laying down and got comfortable, I was looking at the static 
sparks shooting up from the ribbon. At the end of the sparks where the 
ribbon is, where the static spark starts from, there was a shape of a tiny 
ball where the spark came out of. When I rubbed the ribbon where the 
static was shooting up, the static sparks started moving around different 
than normal(normal would mean, positive electricity does not touch each 
other, it always has a little distance from each other). After I started 
to rub the ribbon with the ends of my fingers, up to my first knuckle, 
flat on the ribbon, moving in a sweeping action from right to the left, 
not fast, all the separate shooting static sparks began to pile up like a 
pile of dirt on the ribbon. 






Then all of a sudden(I am not sure of the 
accurate measurement of distance) one spark shot up, about 1", then moved 
over 2" and landed on the ribbon and stayed lit. Immediately another spark 
from that pile of sparks jumped up the same way, arched over and combined 
together with the spark to the left. When both sparks combined together 
that spark got bigger. I gave the ribbon one more sweep to push the sparks 
together on the right side.  Then all of a sudden, one more spark jumped 
up 
from that pile and combined to the left where the 2 sparks combined. Then 
again it became a bigger static spark ball(with making words into a action 
verb (no sound)think of the words "bloop, bloop, bloop, that is what it 
looked like when they combined together). 






As I was looking at this glowing static ball that was floating above the 
ribbon, it started to float up. I put my left hand, with palms up, 
underneath it and it would not lay on the skin of my left palm, it stayed 
about 2' above my palm. AT this point I was scared to call out to anyone 
to tell them to look, cause maybe, it would pop and disappeared with my 
breath.  Then as I moved my same hand up to embrace it, it floated up 
further and then it traveled over to the right side of my hand then slowly 
floated down. I tried to palm it again but I did not succeed, it only kept 
floating down(and I don't mean fast). Then It touched something and 
popped. 






A note: I was laying down flat on the floor with a pillow. Very 
quickly I decided to try again the same thing the same way. And still I 
was afraid to move(sitting up) or talk. I figured I might disturb the 
space around me and move the atoms around enough to push the static 
electricity away. Or my breath would discharge the area..... STARTING 
AGAIN IMMEADETLY, I rubbed the ribbon enough to create static electricity 
sparks, again. The sparks moved around abnormal, they had the same shape 
with the little ball shape connection at the bottom of the shooting spark, 
I sweep the sparks to the left with my fingers with my right hand , they 
piled up then again, one spark shot up the same way and also two others 
followed and it was,"BLOOP, BLOOP, BLOOP". 





The glowing ball separated from the ribbon same as last time with 3 sparks 
an started floating up. I put my left hand under it. Again, it wouldn't 
come in contact with my skin on my palm,(When you take two magnets and try 
to touch both positive sides together, IT WILL NOT HAPPEN, THEY WILL NOT 
TOUCH TOGETHER)...  As it was floating above the palm of my left hand, I 
took my right thumb and index finger making a circle, then positioning the 
circle on my left wrist to measure the diameter of the BALL LIGHNING, so I 
would never forget the size of the POSITIVE STACTIC ELECTRICITY BALL I 
MADE.(4x4 centimeters in size, that's about aprox., all the way across the 
circle; 1 1/2 inches. 






Well the ball again was floating in the atmosphere 
so it started to float off my hand (now realize, the amount of time that 
was passing by, as I was doing doing all this plus the step below. It 
would seem to all add up to about 5-6 seconds, that the ball lightning was 
up in the air. (WOW, I thought).As the ball started to float off my left 
palm, I took my right hand and lowered it to try to push it up from 
underneath to keep it from floating down and poping. I was successful with 
enough movement going real, real, slow, to be able to palm it again and 
keep it above my right palm enough to count to:  one thousand one, one 
thousand two. Then it again floated to the side of my hand, I tried to 
palm it again with my left but I did not succeed, it just floated away 
from my hand then popped on something. 






Sooooo I was hopping I could try again, BUT..... all the static 
electricity was gone. I could not rub anymore static electricity to come 
out of the ribbon... DARN it was fun though. IT TOOK TEN YEARS UNTILL I 
FOUND SOMEONE WHO EXPERENCED BALL LIGHNING. THIS LADY SAID IT CAME OUT OF 
HER TELEVISION. WITH HER CURIOUS AND CONFUSED THOUGHTS, SHE REASEARCHED, 
FINDING OUT IT WAS CALLED "BALL LIGHNING". So I today looked in the web 
and was shocked about what I found. a complete match with that, glowing 
ball *"BALL LIGHTNING"*
