Big John's PFI
Italian & Mediterranean Specialty Foods

A secret among Seattle cooks: Big John's PFI (formerly called Pacific Food Importers outlet store) on 6th Ave S. just north of Royal Brougham Way, near the downtown sports arenas, behind INS in the International District.

See GOOGLE MAP.

An amazing place. Ridiculously low prices on exotic cheese, bulk spices, Euro/Mediterranean/Mideast canned goods. Gol dang! Yerba Mate tea for $2.50/lb instead of $20!!! Smashed shards of European chocolate. Restaurant-quality frozen pitas in bulk. Filo, olives & oil, meats, large collection of pastas (plus frozen stuffed pasta & gnocchi), Mid-east stuff, euro-cookies, etc. Weird mediterranean canned goods. Officially it's Italian/Mediterranean. See their new website for a list of products An old-fashioned grocery store mixed with a bit of '60s warehouse co-op.
