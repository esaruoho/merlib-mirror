New Energy News

The New Energy News (NEN) had been the monthly newsletter for the Institute for New Energy for many years, and its publication was discontinued in 2005. It was emailed every month to all who had paid a $35.00 per year subscription fee to Hal Fox at EEMF in Salt Lake City (see "EEMF" below). All of the monies for the NEN subscriptions had been sent directly to and received by Hal Fox in Salt Lake City.  The NEN had for the past many years been written and published entirely by Patrick Bailey, as Hal Fox had officially resigned all of his INE positions of office, and had requested to be no longer affiliated with the INE in any capacity. Each NEN issue usually contained 20 to 30 pages of current technical information per issue. Emailed versions of the NEN began in June 2000, and published paper versions were mailed out prior that date through the offices of Hal Fox. The New Energy News monthly publication may once again emerge as an INE published newsletter if enough interest for its support in shown in the international scientific community. Past issues are available on CD for $50.00, including free shipping anywhere, via email request through the INE email address, above.
