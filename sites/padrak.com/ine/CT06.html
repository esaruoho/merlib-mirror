IECEC 1997 CT-06 SESSION ANDS PAPERS SCHEDULES

IECEC 1997 CT-06  Conversion Technologies / Innovative Concepts


Four Sessions Are Planned:


Each session will be 3 hours long (for 6 papers),
or 3 1/2 hours long (for 7 papers),
and will probably be scheduled for 9:00 AM - Noon, or 2:00 - 5:00 PM.

Each paper will have 30 minutes exactly, and I mean exactly,
with 20 minutes or so for the presentation,
and 10 minutes or so for questions and answers.

All papers will start and end on time!  No exceptions!
We will have about 10-15 parallel sessions running concurrently,
with attendees attending several papers in different sessions,
and the times of all papers will be listed in the Final Program.

The Final Program Times For Papers will not be changed!
The Final Program will be available at the Conference,
as should be about the same as the Preliminary Program Times.

The scheduled paper times will be available via the IECEC '97 web site at:

     http://members.aol.com/busassist/planning.htm


The four CT-06 Sessions have been announced in the Preliminary Program -
to be held on these days:

Wed. AM, Th. AM, Th. PM, and Fri. AM.

[Wed. PM is Free-Time.]


IECEC '97:  Hilton Hawaiian Village
            Honolulu Hawaii
            July 28 - August 1, 1997
