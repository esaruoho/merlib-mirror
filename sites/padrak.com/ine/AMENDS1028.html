Dear Senators Hatch and Cohen,


We thank you for your April 19 colloquy in which you stated that the 
purpose of S.1028 (approved 4/23) was:

"not to enact a law which would unfairly target alternate medical 
providers."

We also thank you, Senator Hatch, for your floor statement of April 24 in 
which you state:

"It is certainly not my desire, and based on my discussions, nor the intent 
of my colleague Senator Cohen [from Maine] who drafted the original anti-
fraud language, that these provisions in any way impede consumers from 
access to alternate or non-medical treatment therapies."

and:

"I want to assure my colleagues in both the Senate and House - and 
especially those individuals in the alternate and non-medicine community - 
that I will continue my efforts to clarify, where necessary, and FINE tune 
the language as the bill moves to the conference committee."

We are glad to have this floor statement, Senator Hatch, because it 
demonstrates your intention of doing more than just engage in a colloquy 
over this issue with Senator Cohen.  Your floor statement on April 24 tells 
the American People that you HEAR our concerns, and that YOU WILL INDEED 
ACT TO AMEND TITLE V ACCORDINGLY.  We all know that colloquies do not carry 
the force OF THE law, and especially recall the colloquy between Senators 
Symms and Metzenbaum (Congressional Record October 24, 1990) relative TO 
the Nutrition Labeling and Education Act, which was (SUPPOSED) to represent 
the will of CONGRESS with regards to the regulation of dietary supplements 
under that act.

We all remember how the FDA totally and TOTALLY IGNORED that colloquy when 
they drafted their regulations under NLEA, and we are just as certain that 
the FDA, FTC, along with many prosecutors and judges WOULD ALSO IGNORE YOUR 
COLLOQUY while engaging in attacks on alternative medicine if this bill is 
not SUFFICIENTLY ALTERED in the Conference Committee (Life Extension 
Foundation:  http://www.lef.org/lef/index.html).

On April 11, 1996, at the Federation of State Medical Board's meeting in 
Chicago, a tape was made of a work shop titled "Fraudulent Medical 
Practices - Watch and be Wary."  You have been provided with a copy and 
transcript of this tape because it:

Details a clear cut political vendetta against alternative medicine by the 
State Medical Boards, and we know that they WILL USE the vague, unspecific 
fraud language in Title V of S.1028 as passed as a WEAPON against their 
competition:  Alternative Medical Practitioners (Copies of that tape are 
available for $2.00 from 405-634-7855, FAX 405-634-7320).

We therefore INSIST that you work VERY CLOSELY with The Life Extension 
Foundation, The Law Loft, The National Council for Improved Health, and 
other Alternative Medical Organizations to:

AMEND S.1028 WITH SPECIFIC LANGUAGE TO INSURE THAT THIS LEGISLATION WILL

MOST EMPHATICALLY NOT

BE USED TO ATTACK ALTERNATIVE PRACTITIONERS!

The current language implies that such practitioners would face federal 
felony charges and up to seven (7) years in prison.
